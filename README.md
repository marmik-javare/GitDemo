📊 Analysis TypesUnivariate AnalysisFocus: Individual variables.Metrics: Sales distribution, profit spreads, and location frequencies.Plots: Histograms and box plots.Bivariate AnalysisFocus: Relationships between two variables.Metrics: Sales vs. promotions and profit vs. store location.Plots: Scatter plots and grouped bar charts.Multivariate AnalysisFocus: Interactions between three or more variables.Metrics: Profit by product category across different store sizes.
